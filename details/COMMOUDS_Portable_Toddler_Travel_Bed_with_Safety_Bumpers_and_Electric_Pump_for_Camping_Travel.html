COMMOUDS Portable Toddler Travel Bed

A perfect sleep companion for on-the-go families, designed with comfort, safety, and convenience in mind.

Engineered for durability and reliability, this travel bed uses upgraded materials to provide long-lasting use. The 0.3mm PVC construction is reinforced for extra strength, while the 0.5mm high-density flocked surface ensures a soft, cozy feel that mimics a traditional crib.

With 3 one-piece safety bumpers, your toddler is securely protected from rolling off. The sturdy frame supports up to 330 lbs, making it ideal for safe use during family trips, sleepovers, or outdoor adventures.

Featuring an electric air pump, you can inflate the mattress in seconds, making setup and packing effortless. The compact travel bag included ensures easy storage and portability, so you can take this bed anywhere without hassle.

Its removable inner mattress is not only easy to clean but also allows you to customize the bed with your child's favorite bedding items. Lightweight yet functional, this travel bed is built for every adventure, offering restful sleep even in the most unpredictable settings.

Key Features

  • 0.3mm PVC with 0.5mm high-density flocked surface for soft comfort
  • 3-sided safety bumpers (26.6cm / 10.5") to keep your child safe
  • Electric pump for quick inflation and deflation
  • Removable inner mattress for easy cleaning and customization options
  • Compact travel bag for convenient storage and portability

Why Choose COMMOUDS?

Whether you're camping, visiting relatives, or at a hotel, the COMMOUDS Portable Toddler Travel Bed offers a familiar and safe sleep environment. Designed to meet the needs of active parents, it combines practicality with a focus on hygiene, comfort, and ease of use.
