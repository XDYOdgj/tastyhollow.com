Dr. Brown's Lovey Blanket

The Dr. Brown's Lovey Blanket is a must-have for parents who want to give their baby the ultimate comfort. This soft, dual-sided set is designed to aid in self-soothing and create a sense of security for your little one. One side is made of gentle cotton, perfect for wrapping, while the other side features a cozy fuzzy fabric that provides extra warmth and tactile stimulation.

Included in the set are a silicone pacifier and a beaded teether ring. These are ideal for daily use, helping to soothe teething discomfort and offering familiar comfort. The unique design of the pacifier mimics the shape of Dr. Brown's bottle nipple, making it an easy favorite for your baby. The teether ring is perfect for gentle chewing and can be used during sleep or playtime.

With its foldable size and convenient Snap closure, the Lovey Blanket is easy to carry and store. Whether you're at home or out and about, it's the perfect travel companion. This baby essential is machine washable, dishwasher safe (top rack), and sterilizer friendly, ensuring it's always clean and hygienic for your child's safety.
