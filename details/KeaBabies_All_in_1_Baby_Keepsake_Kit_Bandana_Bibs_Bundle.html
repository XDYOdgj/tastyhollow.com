Capture Your Baby's Memories with the KeaBabies All-in-One Keepsake Kit & Bandana Bibs Bundle

The KeaBabies keepsake kit is the perfect way to preserve your baby's most precious moments. Designed to help parents create lasting memories, this bundle includes premium air-dry clay that ensures your baby's hand and footprints are saved for years to come. These keepsakes are ideal for celebrating growth, milestones, and special occasions with loved ones.

What's in the Kit?

  • Premium Smooth-Finish Air-Dry Clay: A high-quality material that keeps your baby's hand and footprints in perfect condition, allowing you to cherish their journey from infancy to toddlerhood.
  • Olive Oil: Included to help maintain the clay's pliability and make it easy to repair or re-knead whenever needed.
  • Elegant Organza Pouch & Gift Box: These add a special touch of sophistication, making the keepsake kit a perfect gift for grandparents, family friends, or new parents.

Why You'll Love the Bandana Bibs?

  • 8-Pack of Super-Absorbent Bandana Bibs: These soft, organic cotton bibs are designed for comfort during feeding, drooling, and teething. Their dual-layered construction includes a durable polyester fleece backing for long-lasting use.
  • Unisex, Versatile Designs: The cloth bibs feature neutral-toned patterns that are perfect for both boys and girls and can be styled with a wide range of outfits, simplifying parenting routines.
  • Machine Washable & Reusable: These practical, easy-to-clean bibs are ideal for daily use, making them a must-have for busy parents.

Whether you're preparing for a baby shower, holiday gift-giving, or just looking to create a heartfelt keepsake, the KeaBabies bundle offers a unique combination of sentimental value and essential baby care items.
