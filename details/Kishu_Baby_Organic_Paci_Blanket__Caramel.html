Kishu Baby Organic Paci Blanket - Caramel

This Kishu Baby Organic Paci Blanket is designed with your baby's comfort and convenience in mind. Crafted from premium organic cotton muslin, it's gentle on sensitive skin while offering a cozy, soothing embrace. With four layers of soft fabric, it's ideal for use during naps, car rides, or anytime your little one needs a secure and calming presence.

Featuring a built-in pacifier clip and secure snap closure, this blanket keeps your baby's pacifier safely within reach, eliminating the risk of loss or contamination.
