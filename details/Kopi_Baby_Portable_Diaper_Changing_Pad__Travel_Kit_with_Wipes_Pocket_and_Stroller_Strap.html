Kopi Baby Portable Diaper Changing Pad

The perfect companion for parents who are always on the move. This lightweight and compact changing pad is designed to fit seamlessly in your diaper bag or travel gear, offering a safe and clean surface for your baby wherever you go.

Featuring a built-in pillow, this pad provides added comfort and support, making every diaper change gentle and stress-free.
