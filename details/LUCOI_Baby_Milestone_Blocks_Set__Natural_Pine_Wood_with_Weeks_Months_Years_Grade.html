LUCOI Baby Milestone Blocks Set

A beautiful way to document your baby's growth and milestones with style and functionality.

Every parent dreams of capturing the precious journey of their baby's first year, and the LUCOI Baby Milestone Blocks Set is designed to help. Meticulously crafted from solid natural pine wood with a gentle rustic white finish, these blocks are built to last and are entirely safe for little hands. The set includes two square blocks and one rectangular block, offering a versatile solution for tracking weeks, months, years, and even grade levels as your child grows.

Each block is coated with non-toxic, baby-safe paint, ensuring a worry-free addition to your nursery or baby shower setup. Whether you're creating a timeline of your child's early achievements or simply looking for a decorative touch, these blocks are a great way to add charm and intentionality to your space. More than just a toy, it's a meaningful keepsake that grows with your child, preserving memories for years to come.
