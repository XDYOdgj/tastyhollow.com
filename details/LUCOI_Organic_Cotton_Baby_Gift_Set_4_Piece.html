LUCOI Organic Cotton Baby Gift Set - A Thoughtful 4-Piece Bundle for Newborns

Treat new parents to a cozy and stylish gift with our 4-piece organic cotton baby set. Perfect for baby showers, welcome boxes, or any special occasion, it's designed with care to provide comfort, safety, and endless play value for your little one.

Crafted with Organic Cotton Muslin

Every item in this set is made with 100% organic cotton muslin, ensuring it's soft, breathable, and safe for your baby's delicate skin. The natural fibers are gentle, hypoallergenic, and perfect for helping your baby feel cozy and secure from the start.

Perfect for Any Newborn

With a combination of practical and playful elements, this set includes a bunny ears teether ring, a reversible bandana bib, a whimsical bunny muslin lovey, and a wooden announcement card. These items are ideal for soothing, feeding, and creating lasting memories for your baby.

Gender Neutral and Timeless Design

Our LUCOI gift set is designed with a gender-neutral aesthetic, making it an excellent choice for baby showers and welcome gifts. The charming rainbow prints and adorable bunny motifs add a playful touch without being too specific, so it's versatile for any little one.

Safe and Non-Toxic Materials

Designed with the safety of your baby in mind, all products are free from harmful chemicals and toxic substances. The wooden announcement card is carefully crafted for durability, while the teether ring is designed for safe relief as your baby's teeth develop.

Premium Packaging for a Memorable Gift

Each item is beautifully packaged and ready to give, making it an ideal addition to any baby registry or a heartfelt gift for new parents. The set is easy to gift and comes with everything included, saving you time and effort.

A Versatile Choice for Every Occasion

Whether you're looking for a baby shower gift box, a newborn welcome gift, or a thoughtful gift for a special occasion like Christmas or Easter, this set is designed to deliver both style and functionality. It's the perfect way to show your love and care for a new family.
