M8 Quarter Round Spindle Rod Adapter 2 Pack

Enhance your home safety with these sleek and functional adapters designed specifically for baby and pet gates. Ideal for use with quarter round shoe moulding, they offer a secure, no-drill installation without altering your walls.

Key Features

  • Curved design fits snugly around baseboard shoe moulding, ensuring a seamless look.
  • Universal fit for 3/4" (2cm) quarter round, suitable for most common installations.
  • Extra long length of 4.75" for easy access to tight corners and hard-to-reach areas.
  • 8mm size designed for pressure mounted baby gates, providing a strong and stable hold.
  • Compatible with major safety gate brands such as Regalo, Summer Infant, Carlson, Top Paw, and Evenflo.

Why Choose This Adapter?

Whether you're installing a baby gate or a pet gate, these adapters offer a hassle-free solution. Their white finish blends effortlessly with most interior designs, maintaining the aesthetic of your home while boosting safety. With a 2-pack format, you're always prepared for multiple gate setups or replacements.

Versatile and Reliable

If you're looking for an 8mm hole size, this M8 adapter is the perfect fit. For added versatility, consider the larger M10 size if your gate requires a bigger mounting hole. These spindle rod adapters are a must for any family or pet owner seeking safe, stylish, and simple gate solutions.
