MYPIN Retractable Baby Gate

A smart, versatile safety solution for modern families

This retractable baby gate is designed to keep your little one safe in homes with stairs, hallways, and doorways. Its extra wide 55\" (140cm) size ensures a perfect fit for most entryways, making it ideal for both indoor and outdoor use. The flexible retractable system allows easy adjustment in different angles, offering adaptability to suit your home layout.

With a detachable design, you can move the gate between rooms or areas without the need for reinstallation. The smart close feature automatically locks at 30 degrees, giving you peace of mind and preventing unintended access by babies or pets. Plus, the one-handed operation makes locking and unlocking simple, even when you're managing baby essentials or carrying groceries around.

Key Features
  • Extra wide 55\" (140cm) design for large doorways and hallways
  • Retractable and adjustable for flexible use in different areas
  • Detachable with included hardware for easy relocation
  • Smart close feature prevents accidental opening
  • One-handed operation for convenient locking and unlocking
Why Choose This Gate?

Whether you're keeping babies safe from stairs or preventing pets from wandering through hallways, this gate delivers secure protection without the hassle of fixed installations. The ground buckle design ensures a firm grip, keeping your family safe and secure. Combined with a durable aluminum alloy roll mandrel, this gate is built to last while maintaining a smooth and reliable operation for everyday use.
