Stephan Baby Boo-Bonnie & Boo Cube Teether Set

The Stephan Baby Boo-Bonnie & Boo Cube Teether Set is a delightful duo designed to bring comfort and relief to your baby during the teething stage. Soft and gentle, this set combines functionality with charm to make teething a less stressful experience.

Key Features

  • The soft, 3.5" x 2" Boo-Bonnie offers a plush feel that's perfect for gentle teething relief and soothing during playtime.
  • The Boo Cube is pre-filled with distilled water, delivering a cooling sensation that eases discomfort and provides a refreshing teething aid.
  • Both pieces are made from 100% cotton and BPA-free plastic, ensuring safety for your baby's sensitive skin and teeth.
  • Easy to clean with either machine wash or top rack dishwasher options, making it simple to maintain hygiene.
  • Designed with rounded edges for a safe chew experience, and built to last through daily use.

Why Parents Love It

Combining comfort and cooling relief, this teether set is an essential tool for parents looking to support their little one's development. The pretty Blue Gingham design adds a whimsical touch, making it a favorite for both baby and parent alike. With durable construction and safe materials, it's built to last and trusted by countless families.
