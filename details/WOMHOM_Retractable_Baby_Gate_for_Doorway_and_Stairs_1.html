WOMHOM Retractable Baby Gate

Designed for modern families, the WOMHOM Retractable Baby Gate provides a safe, flexible solution to protect your home and loved ones. Whether you're managing a playful toddler or keeping your pets secure, this innovative gate is built to adapt to your needs. The soft yet durable mesh is gentle on skin and strong enough to block access to potentially dangerous areas, ensuring your children and pets stay safe without limiting your freedom.

With its retractable design, this gate fits a wide range of openings from 0 to 60 inches, making it perfect for doorways, stairs, and other entry points throughout your home. Its one-handed operation lets you easily open the gate while holding your child or managing multiple tasks, and it's designed so little hands can't accidentally trigger it. This convenience and security make it an essential addition to any family space.

The no-threshold design ensures smooth movement and prevents tripping hazards, especially when moving with groceries or small children. It securely installs on walls using screws, offering a reliable barrier that meets strict international safety standards. This gate is the smart choice for parents and pet owners who want peace of mind without sacrificing style or functionality.
