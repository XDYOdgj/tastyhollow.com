Zanxiantu Baby Lounger Cover

Add a touch of whimsy and comfort to your baby's playtime with this adorable donut-shaped lounger cover. Crafted from a breathable and soft fabric, it's gentle on your baby's delicate skin and ensures a cozy, safe environment for lounging and resting.

Its zipper-free envelope closure is designed with safety in mind, eliminating the risk of injury from zippers and making it ideal for babies who may chew or pull on them.
