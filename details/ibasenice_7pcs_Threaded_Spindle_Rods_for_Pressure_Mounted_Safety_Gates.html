Upgrade your home safety system or repair a damaged pet gate with these high-quality threaded spindle rods. Designed for universal compatibility, they fit most standard safety gates and pet barriers, making them a versatile choice for various installations.

  • Universal Fit: Compatible with most standard safety gates and pet barriers, ensuring a perfect match for your needs.
